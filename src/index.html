Instructions: Setting up the study

  • Set the volume on your computer to about 25%.
  • After you set the volume, click the Test1 button to play a test sound.
  • If the sound is distorted or so loud it hurts your ears, decrease the volume on your computer.
  • If the sound is softer than conversation, increase the volume on your computer.
  • Complete this process until the sound is moderately soft, or the volume setting is at 100%.
  • Then, click the Done button.

  • Important Notes:
  • Wear your earphones so that the sound plays in your ear with the weaker tinnitus.
  • If you have tinnitus in both ears, listen to the sound in your right ear.

  • Please do not adjust the volume on your computer again
  • even if you cannot hear the sounds later in the experiment.
