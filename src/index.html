Tinnitus Testing

Instructions: Setting up the study

  • Set the volume on your computer to about 25%.
  • After you set the volume, click the Test button to play a test sound.
  • If the sound is distorted or so loud it hurts your ears, decrease the volume on your computer.
  • If the sound is softer than a loud conversation, increase the volume on your computer.
  • Complete this process until you find a level that is moderately loud, or the volume setting is at 100%. Then, click the Done button.
